About Gender and Climate Change

  • Global Challenges: Gender equality and climate change are two urgent global issues.
  • Marginalized Impact: Marginalized populations, especially women and girls, are disproportionately affected by climate change.
  • Resource Inequality: Women often have unequal access to opportunities, resources, and education, which increases their vulnerability to climate impacts.
  • Improved Solutions: Reducing these disparities is essential for more effective climate solutions and gender equality.
  • Our Initiative: Using data and cutting-edge technologies, we tackle both gender inequality and climate change simultaneously.
  • Women's Engagement: By involving women in climate action, we can accelerate the transition to environmental sustainability.
  • Call to Action: Join us in bridging the gap between environmental stewardship and gender equality to create a more equitable future.

Objectives

  • End Discrimination and Violence: Eliminate discrimination and violence against girls and women globally.
  • Promote Women in Leadership: Ensure equal leadership opportunities for women in climate decision-making.
  • Access to Health Rights: Guarantee universal access to sexual and reproductive health rights.
  • Build Climate Resilience: Enhance community resilience to climate change and integrate mitigation strategies into national policies.
